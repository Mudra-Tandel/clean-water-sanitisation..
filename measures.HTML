Measures for Water & Sanitation

Precautions:
  • Use water purification systems
  • Avoid open defecation
  • Ensure proper sewage and drainage systems
  • Practice handwashing with clean water
  • Promote rainwater harvesting
  • Use eco-friendly sanitation methods

1.Causes of the Crisis The crisis stems from a combination of environmental, infrastructural, and social factors Outreach International
*Climate change: Altered rainfall patterns, glacial melt, and intensified droughts reduce available freshwater
*Population growth and urbanization: Rising demand outpaces infrastructure, particularly in developing regions
*Pollution: Industrial waste, untreated sewage, and agricultural runoff contaminate rivers, lakes, and groundwater
*Over-extraction of groundwater: Unsustainable agricultural and industrial use leads to declining water tables, affecting millions daily
*Inadequate infrastructure and governance: Many water systems fail due to lack of skilled professionals or sustainable management, leaving communities with unreliable water access
